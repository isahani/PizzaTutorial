Easy Homemade Pizza Sauce Recipe:-


INGREDIENTS

6 oz tomato paste (1 -6 oz can) ▢15 oz tomato sauce (1 - 15 oz can) ▢1-2 TBS oregano to taste ▢2 TBS Italian seasoning ▢½ tsp garlic powder ▢½ tsp onion powder ▢½ TBS garlic salt ▢¼ tsp freshly ground black pepper ▢1 tsp sugar* coconut sugar for paleo or omit


INSTRUCTIONS

Mix tomato paste and sauce together in a medium size bowl until well combined (all the lumps of paste are incorporated into the sauce). Add the rest of the ingredients (oregano, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, garlic salt, pepper and sugar) and mix well! Use in your favorite pizza recipe!
